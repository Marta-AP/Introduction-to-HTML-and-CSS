There is not much to know about me. My name is Marta, I come from Spain and have been living with my partner in Odense, Denmark, for two years. I am an English philologist, a nerd, a gamer, a writer without inspiration... Many years ago, I practiced enduro. I love traveling —if I have the time and the money—, cooking for those I love and I hygger mig watching a good fantasy or sci-fi movie. Since September 2022, I am attending SDU in Kolding. So, I am currently a student, again.

Since I moved to Denmark, I cycle a lot and take pictures of every outstanding corner I bump into. It was then that I opened my Instagram account. There, I shared pictures of my summer holiday 2021 around North Jutland. It was short and I did not have the time to visit the whole area, but it was magical. For this reason, I want to share it with you all.

So, please, be my guest and travel to this amazing area with me.
